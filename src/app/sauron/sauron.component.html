Enabling company workflow metrics

Brief

I was asked by Head stakeholders to design an application that enables them to be able to overview important company internal metrics.

Cons

  • Wasn’t aloud to waste anyones time.
  • Wasn’t aloud to interview the executives through the research phase.

Research

After kicking off the initial start of the project, the first thing I wanted to do was approach the potential users and ask questions. As I wasn’t allowed to approach the users, I thought the next big thing would be to contact the PA or someone that may work close to that teams stakeholder.

Questions asked,

  • What sort of metrics do you guys oversee every day?
  • Whats the most common metric related question there stakeholders ask?
  • What tools and applications are used track metrics?

API Access

After finalising the information I started to try and figure out data points, as well as see if it was possible to collect these important metrics via existing individual teams applications API’s.

  • Data around website metrics
  • Our internal development process and status

After Reviewing

After gathering all the opertunities and the data points I started to create individual persona’s for each category of executive that would make use of this dashboard

User-testing

After designing the wireframes/low-fi prototype I ventured to test internal users

Questions I asked !

  • What they liked about the application so far!
  • What data points they liked!
  • What could I do to improve the exp?
Please keep in mind this is my first dashboard design and practice makes perfect :)
After conducting the user tests, research and whole lot of Itterations I had a final MVP I could present to the Stakeholders for feedback

Feedback Given.

Feedback Given

  • Doesn’t show percentages of developer resources and we want this!
  • White is really strong on the eyes and hard to visibly see the data
  • Mentioned they didn’t notice the Set and Delivered tasks data
  • Wanted to see a number of tasks in DOR “Definition of ready
  • Wanted to see not just current but past sprint information
  • Wants to see a clearer progress bar in the sprint section
  • Wants to know what tasks have been replaced
  • hey wanted to see the new sprint name not old…
Anyways after itterations, consultations and constant research! I managed to design a final product that the executives were happy with !
